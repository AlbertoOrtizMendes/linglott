About us

Linglott was born after two friends from different parts of the world realised the amount of struggle that goes to learning a new language and adapting to a different culture.

A struggle that doesn’t involve grammar rules or vocabulary memorisation, but one that involves having a hard time figuring out the best way to learn and where the best and most importantly, accessible teachers are. After spending a couple years trying different solutions, they realised that enough is enough and then Linglott was created to fill this gap.

Our mission is to allow people from all over the world to immerse themselves into a different culture.

Our vison is to be the go-to place for students and companies looking to learn another language or another culture.
